Moving helpers to React
The situation with helpers is unfortunate. Helpers are generally the least object oriented part of rails
technological stack. We can move helpers methods into our objects (but we shouldn’t if their concern
is related to presentation and not business logic), we can move them into presenters responsible for
building the JSON. Or… we can move them to our react components. Closer to our view’s logic.
