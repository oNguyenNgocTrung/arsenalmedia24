Summary
React gets along well with most of the external tools and libraries you may want to use. Handling
events in React is all about no surprises - you get a very simple mechanism for binding events.
It is similar to the flow you know well from the pure DOM or jQuery. With this knowledge, you
can create even more complex components. Also, you can integrate the technologies you love with
React.
