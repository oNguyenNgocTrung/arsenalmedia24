If the flash notice appears at the beginning or if it is added later, we make it slide from top to bottom
(from -50px to 0px) and we make it become visible (from 0.01 opacity to 1).
When the message disappears the animation reverses the transition in: the notification slides from
bottom to top and becomes invisible.
