Tips: Removing duplication in
handlers
When you have multiple handlers which look similar (like it happened in our Meetup example) it
might be a good idea to refactor them into one. Here are some ideas on how to do it.
This is our starting point:
CreateNewMeetupForm = React.createClass
titleChanged: (event) ->
@setState(title: event.target.value)
descriptionChanged: (event) ->
@setState(description: event.target.value)
render: ->
DOM.form
id: "my-form"
React.DOM.input
name: "title"
onChange: @titleChanged
React.DOM.input
name: "description"
onChange: @descriptionChanged
