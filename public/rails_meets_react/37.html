you can use
<button
type="button">
when you
don’t want the browser to do anything
by itself when click occurred
. It’s short, supported by the browsers and you don’t need to use
event.preventDefault()
with it. You might find it useful in some situations.
But forms should rely on
onSubmit
, not
onClick
for individual buttons. Otherwise we would
be breaking other ways to submit the form (mainly hitting return when in a text field) and thus
diminishing accessibility for no real gain. (Kudos to Jarkko Laine for this feedback!).
